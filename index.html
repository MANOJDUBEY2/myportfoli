Understanding Language

"My focused study in NLP enables me to not just analyse numerical data, but also leverage textual context for comprehensive automated decision-making — transforming unstructured business language into structured, actionable intelligence."

Most data scientists stop at numbers. I go further — because real-world business data is 80% unstructured text: emails, reports, contracts, customer feedback. By combining classical ML with NLP, I build systems that understand the full context of a business situation, not just its metrics.
